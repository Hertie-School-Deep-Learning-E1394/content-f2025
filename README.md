Assistant: Alex Karras (karras@hertie-school.org)

## Course overview

As the fastest growing subfield of machine learning, deep learning is the technology behind facial recognition, machine translation, ChatGPT, and many other applications. In this course, students learn the main theoretical concepts of (deep) neural networks and get introduced to applications in computer vision, natural language processing, and other areas. Students gain hands-on experience by training and testing their own models in policy-relevant applications.
